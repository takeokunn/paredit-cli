# Code of Conduct

`paredit-cli` is maintained as a professional engineering project. Maintainers,
contributors, and users are expected to keep technical discussion direct,
respectful, and evidence-based.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus on the code, behavior, and reproducible evidence instead of personal
  attacks.
- Assume mistakes can be corrected; explain problems concretely and propose a
  better path when possible.
- Keep issue reports, reviews, and support threads relevant to the repository.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles while maintaining
  a high technical bar.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Repeatedly derailing technical threads with personal conflict or spam.
- Publishing private information about other participants without permission.
- Bad-faith bug reports, benchmarks, or security claims presented as evidence.

## Moderation

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull
requests, and other contributions that violate this policy. Repeated or severe
violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

For conduct problems in public project spaces, contact the maintainer through
GitHub and include links, screenshots, or other concrete evidence. For
security-sensitive problems, use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.
